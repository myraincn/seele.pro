Deepening Global Influence — Seele in Huobi Carnival of South Korea

2018-08-02


From August 2nd to 3rd, Seele team is invited to participate in the Huobi Carnival at Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas in Seoul, South Korea. This move is intended to deepen Seele’s influence in Korea and to strengthen the interaction among the Korean and the global communities.

Dr. Maolin Zheng, Dr. Wei Bi and Elias wish success to Huobi Carnival



Hotel Outdoor Scene




Carnival Main Venue


Huobi Carnival is an annual representative promotion activity for Huobi exchange, where the Chairman of Huobi Korea Guofeng Zhao, and Bitman founder Wu Jihan, who rarely attends any market activity, will also participate in this event, besides Seele chief scientist Dr. Wei Bi and Seele Asian Ecosyetem Leader Elias.

In this Huobi Carnival event, Seele team has prepared a lot of news to announce. We believe that investors will be very excited.


Seele Exhibition Area




Seele Showcase Theme


On August 2, the industry landing project mentioned by Dr. Wei Bi in the community Q&A will have its official debut. Not only that, more information will be publicized on the medical industry application that has been mentioned before.

In the morning of August 3, Dr. Wei Bi will deliver a keynote speech titled “Born for Industry”, during which he will officially announce the specific contents of the previously mentioned Yellow Paper. Later in the afternoon, Dr. Wei Bi will conduct a live broadcast with He Huang in Bitcoin World to discuss the market performance and future planning of Seele. More details of the Yellow Paper and industry landing will be announced in the live broadcast.

On the evening of August 3, Dr. Wei Bi will have an in-depth communication with many representatives from the Korean market, to present the three technical concepts of Seele to local Korean industry, capital and community representatives, and further introduce the industry landing application.

The participation in the Huobi Carnival is an important step in the ecological construction of Seele, and a significant opportunity for Korean community and investors to recognize Seele . More details regarding the Carnival event will be announced in the follow-up.
