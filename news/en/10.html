Seele Main Net Officially Launched!

2019-03-31


A Special Thank You

With the official release of the Seele main net, everyone in the Seele team would like to thank each and everyone of you that’s supporting us along the way. Since the inception of the project, we have been working feverishly in developing our vision of the blockchain 4.0 technologies, such as EDA, heterogeneous forest network etc. With the collaborative knowledge of the team and the help of everyone, we are proud to announce the release of the Seele main net, the success has only begun!

Our mission is to build an advanced blockchain network that could power the industries with real world applications. Our goal is to use the unique abilities of blockchain to solve problems with efficiency and transparency. With the release of Seele version 1.0, we have laid a solid foundation for Seele’s growth. Seele Net is a blockchain network powered by our innovative sPow algorithm that is ASIC resistant with solid sharding capability and excellent TPS performance.

We wanted to thank our entire network of beta-testers who were able to keep this quiet and out of the eye of the public until just recently. You were instrumental in helping us achieve a momentous day today.
