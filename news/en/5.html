Seele becomes the first project for CoinEx accelerator restart

2019-04-10


After the digital currency exchange CoinEx accelerator restarts, the first project, Seele, will start to accelerate at 22:00 on April 10. The Seele ERC20 token has already launched at the CoinEx exchange, which will provide users with low-priced purchasing opportunities at below-market prices. It is reported that CoinEx will jointly support the Seele main network with Seele communities and exchange assets for users. The CoinEx Accelerator service will be open to all premium blockchain projects on the market and not limited to first-time market entry projects.
