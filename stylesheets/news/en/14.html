Seele Born For Industry

2018-08-08


From August 2 to August 3, Seele was invited to participate in the Huobi Carnival held in Seoul. At this event, Dr. Wei Bi announced the latest progress in Seele’s R&D and industrial applications. In his keynote speech ‘Seele — Born For Industry’, Dr. Wei Bi gave a detailed introduction of Seele’s ecosystem and strategic outlook.

Vertical Solution for Healthcare Industry is Going to Launch

By dividing ownership and usage rights of patients’ medical data, the vertical solution for healthcare industry could bring significant help to resolve hospitals’ pain points regarding the trusted exchange of data among various of agencies. The medical project of Seele aims to provide a ground-breaking solution for cerebrovascular diseases during the ‘golden time’ of emergency treatment.

According to Dr. Wei Bi, this medical use case of Seele’s ecosystem will dramatically improve the status quo of sharing of medical data. Medical professionals will be provided with a secure, stable, and convenient auxiliary diagnosis system. Patients will benefit from the intelligent and convenient healthcare management platform. In short, the solution will be mutually beneficial for both the patients and doctors by providing a superior and trust-worthy treatment environment.
